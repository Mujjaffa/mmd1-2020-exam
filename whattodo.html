WHAT TO DO

One of the biggest problems of the climate change, is that nobody doesn’t really know what to do. People can’t stop the factories from producing pollution, people can’t stop cars from driving. But it’s the small everyday stuff that can be a beginning of the end. Instead of throwing your bottle in the ocean or the woods, you should recycle them, stop buying waste and look after environmentally friendly products.

  • AVOID FLYING
