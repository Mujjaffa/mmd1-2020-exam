Our cause

We created BLUEREEF after learning how much pollution, our oceans are going through. We learned that our oceans are dying and creating a big problem for not only us, but the whole eco-system of the ocean, millions of lives and plants are disappearing every day, without people knowing.

A TIME FOR CHANGE

Coral Reefs

With the rising water levels & temperature of the world oceans, the effect on the coral reefs is devastating, as coral reefs are a huge part of the underwater ecosystem. Creating oxygen & life around the world oceans.

What to do

One of the biggest problems of the climate change, is that nobody doesn’t really know what to do. People can’t stop the factories from producing pollution, people can’t stop cars from driving. But it’s the small everyday stuff that can be a beginning of the end.
